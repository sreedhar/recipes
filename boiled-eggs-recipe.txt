Take the desired number of eggs.
Get a container which can hold all the eggs and still have some extra room on top
Put the eggs in container.
Add water to container so that the level is above the eggs.
Check if any egg is floating, if you find any discard them as they are stale
Put the container on a stove top and boil until after 10 min when the water starts to boil.
Drain and add colde water
Peel eggs -- they are boiled.
